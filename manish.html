Sign-In and Sign-Up guidelines for candidates:-

Guidelines for New candidates:-

  1. New candidates who have not registered for intake 01/2022 are required to click on New User?Register link and fill the Sign Up details.

  2. Enter OTP received on Email Id and SMS(only for country code (+91)) for verification.

  3. After successful verification of Sign Up details a system generated password will be sent to their registered Email Id.

  4. After filling Sign Up details candidate will be redirected again to the Sign In page where he is required to fill his registered email id and system generated password which is sent to his registered Email Id.

  5. After providing the Email Id and system generated password, candidate will be redirected to a new page where he has to reset a new password of his own choice.

  6. After setting his own personal password he will be redirected to Sign In page where he should login with the registered Email Id and new password as decided by the candidate.
